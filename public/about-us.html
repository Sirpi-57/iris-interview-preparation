About IRIS

Your AI-powered interview coach that helps you prepare, practice, and perform better in job interviews.

Our Mission

At IRIS, we're on a mission to democratize interview preparation and level the playing field for job seekers worldwide. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality interview training, regardless of their background, connections, or resources.

Through our AI-powered platform, we aim to eliminate interview anxiety, build confidence, and ensure that candidates can authentically showcase their skills and potential during the job interview process.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no qualified candidate misses out on their dream job due to interview anxiety or lack of preparation. IRIS strives to become the gold standard in interview readiness, helping millions of job seekers globally to present their best selves during interviews.

By combining advanced AI technology with proven interview techniques, we're creating a future where interview success is determined by a candidate's actual abilities and potential, not their interviewing skills alone.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at IRIS

Accessibility

We're committed to making professional interview preparation accessible to everyone, with options for all budgets and needs.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible with AI to create the most realistic and helpful interview experience.

Effectiveness

Every feature we build is designed to deliver measurable improvement in our users' interview performance.
