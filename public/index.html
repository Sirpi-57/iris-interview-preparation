Beat the ATS, Impress Recruiters

Did you know most large companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes? A generic resume often gets rejected before a human even sees it.

Every job description has specific keywords and requirements. Your resume needs to reflect these to get noticed.

IRIS helps you:

  • Analyze your resume against ANY job description.
  • Identify missing keywords and skill gaps relevant to the ATS.
  • Get suggestions to tailor your resume effectively for each application.
  • Increase your chances of passing the initial screening.

1 AI Resume Builder

Create professional, ATS-optimized resumes with our intuitive interface. Input your details section by section with our guided approach.

Generate professional resumes that consistently score high on Applicant Tracking Systems. Our AI ensures optimal keyword matching and formatting for your target role.

Download your polished resume as a ready-to-submit PDF file that passes through ATS filters with high scores, increasing your chances of getting that interview call.

3 Resume Match Analysis

Upload your resume and the job description to receive a detailed match analysis with percentage scores to understand how well you fit the position.

Identify key strengths and skill gaps specifically tailored to your target role. Our system highlights areas like technical skills, project experience, and certifications that need attention.

Access a curated list of technical and behavioral questions you're likely to encounter based on the job description and your profile.

Each question comes with preparation tips and framework suggestions to help you craft compelling answers that highlight your expertise and experience.

5 Addressing Skill Gaps

Receive targeted strategies to positively frame areas where your profile might differ from the job description requirements.

Get personalized advice on how to discuss when questions are asked over the skill gaps, with specific talking points to emphasize your transferable skills and learning capacity.

6 Personalized Prep Timeline

Generate a customized day-by-day study schedule based on your interview date and the skill gaps identified during resume analysis.

Each day is structured with morning, afternoon, and evening activities focused on critical areas and technical skills tailored to your role.

7 AI Mock Interviews

Practice with our AI interviewer in a realistic interview environment with technical, behavioral, and general questions tailored to your target role.

Develop confidence through repeated practice sessions, with options to focus on specific question types or areas where you need improvement.

8 Interview Performance Analysis

Review comprehensive analysis of your interview performance with scores across technical, communication, and behavioral dimensions.

See detailed breakdowns of your strengths and areas for improvement in each category, with specific feedback on technical accuracy, communication clarity, and response structure.

9 Improvement Roadmap

Receive targeted advice on how to improve specific aspects of your interview performance, from technical depth to communication clarity and STAR method usage.

Each improvement area comes with practical exercises and suggested practice methods to help you make measurable progress before your next interview.

10 AI Tutor Guidance

Review model answers for every question asked during your mock interview, with detailed explanations of what makes a strong response.

Each suggested answer includes the rationale behind it, helping you understand not just what to say but why it works, improving your ability to craft compelling responses to any question.

11 Progress Tracking

Monitor your improvement over time with detailed progress charts showing gains across multiple interviews and practice sessions.

Track specific improvement metrics in technical knowledge, communication skills, and behavioral responses, with clear visualization of your growth in each area.

12 Teacher Dashboard

For educational institutions, department representatives and professors can monitor student resume analyses to identify weaknesses and track interview performance.

The dashboard provides aggregated and individual student data, allowing educators to offer targeted guidance and support to help students improve specific areas before real interviews.
